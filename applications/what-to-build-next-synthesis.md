# What to Build Next Project Synthesis (Productization & Tooling Phase)

**Status**: Ingested via Lucid Judgment audit (2026-04-05)  
**Confidence**: Medium-High (78 %)  
**Disconfirmers**: Multiple new concepts (BHA, TIRP, VeriVerse, Symmetry Audit Protocol) introduced without independent wiki corroboration; no shipped product metrics

## Overview
The "What to Build Next" project (primarily Mar–Apr 2026) translates Heuristic Algebra from theoretical scaffolds into deployable products, tools, and commercial pathways. It builds directly on the pre-wiki Heuristics laboratory phase.

## Core Outputs
- **Productization Framework**: "Productize Ruthlessly" strategy; explicit operator selectors ($\oplus_{\text{ha}}$ / $\oplus_{\text{bio}}$); open-source non-core while guarding algebraic core; licensing of high-Elo scaffolds under Ayn Rand Lens.
- **Operational Tools**:
  - AxiomChat: Multi-LLM heuristic-aware chatbot with persona/mental-model attachment and metrics tracking.
  - Forecast-Informed Invention Journal: Predictive journaling with rotating LLM forecasts and compounding metrics.
  - Voice agents (Lucid Thinker / Agent Reasoner): Hands-free iOS/desktop prototypes via Shortcuts + Grok API.
  - Local RAG and synthetic-data generators for heuristic workflows.
- **Scaffold Extensions**: Forecasting (Polyphonic Drift Score), Ideas ($\neg$-heavy), AdaptiveGD (BHA), Symmetry Audit Protocol v1.
- **Commercial & Application Directions**: Personas/mental models rules; ethical recombination; innovation pipelines (TIRP, VeriVerse); five value-extraction paths (SaaS, licensing, etc.).

**Known Disconfirmers**: AdaptiveGD (BHA), Symmetry Audit Protocol v1, TIRP, and VeriVerse are introduced here without prior wiki mention — their status and validity are unverifiable from existing wiki content. The "Ayn Rand Lens" licensing model is referenced but not defined in the wiki.
