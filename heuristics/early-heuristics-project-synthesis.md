## Overview
The Heuristics project (Oct 2025–Apr 2026) constituted the foundational development of Heuristic Algebra prior to the formal LLM-Wiki. It established HA as a Euclidean-style formal system for heuristics and produced the first scaffolds, operator applications, and domain sets later validated in H1/H2 campaigns.

## Core Outputs
- **Heuristic Sets**: Inference (INF1–INF7), novelty detection (five first-principles heuristics), problem-solving (Feynman integration + principle excision), critical thinking, investing (Fed 2 % target analysis), and ethical frameworks recombined via $\oplus$ and $\neg$.
- **Scaffold Lineage**: Lucid Judgment ($\oplus_{\text{ha}}$ of SJ/DC/RR) achieved top Elo; Forecasting and Ideas Scaffolds prototyped with Polyphonic Drift Score and negation-heavy creativity.
- **Experimental Foundations**: Elo tournaments, Brier scoring, stationarity metrics, and synthetic-data protocols directly feeding current Campaign H1/H2 results.
- **Product & Application Directions**: Personas/mental models rules; open-source non-core strategy; licensing model; AxiomChat and Forecast-Informed Invention Journal prototypes.
