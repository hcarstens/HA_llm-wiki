## Judgment

**Confidence**: Medium-High (75 %). The key insight is structurally sound — the ingest chain (laboratory → wiki → productization) is well-documented. However, the "post-training intelligence multiplier" claim is aspirational; none of the proposed experiments have been run, and all three reference tools/protocols that remain at prototype or specification stage.

**Known Disconfirmers**: AxiomChat, Polyphonic Drift Score, and Symmetry Audit Protocol v1 are all uncorroborated in the wiki (flagged in the what-to-build-next-synthesis page). The proposed Elo benchmark for AxiomChat assumes a deployed product that does not yet exist. No evidence that the "self-correcting discipline" claimed in the key insight actually survives the transition from wiki to production tooling.
